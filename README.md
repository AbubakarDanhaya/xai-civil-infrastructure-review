# Explainable AI for Civil Infrastructure Management

This repository accompanies the review paper:

**"Explainable Artificial Intelligence in Civil Infrastructure Management: Review of Applications, Trends, and Opportunities."**

## Overview

This project provides a comprehensive review of Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) in civil infrastructure management. It explores how XAI techniques improve the transparency, interpretability, and trustworthiness of AI models across infrastructure planning, monitoring, maintenance, and decision-making.

## Objectives

- Review current applications of XAI in civil infrastructure.
- Identify emerging research trends and commonly used methods.
- Analyze existing challenges and research gaps.
- Highlight future opportunities for researchers and practitioners.

## Repository Status

🚧 Work in progress.

The repository will be updated throughout the review process with literature, notes, figures, bibliometric analyses, and supporting materials.
